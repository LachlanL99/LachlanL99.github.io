About

My background is in economics. After graduating with a degree in economics from the University of Cambridge in 2021, I began working as an economic consultant. Outside of work, I look to improve my coding skills through projects I find interesting.

My work as an economic consultant involves data and drafting work, as well as project management. I have been involved with cartel damages cases requiring econometric analysis and expert report drafting, a number of domestic and international merger and acquisition cases with large datasets and in-depth data cleaning, and litigation cases drawing upon presentation skills. Across these projects I have worked in teams of different sizes, and have been given responsibility over workstreams and management of more junior colleagues.

I have business-standard language skills in French and Chinese.

The intention of this project portfolio website is to share my work, collaborate with others, and explore options in the coding world.
